# Parul University Smart Robot Assistant

This project is a smart robot software idea for Parul University that works like a friendly college mentor and guide. The robot will communicate with students, visitors, and parents in a natural and helpful way. The robot name is Deno, and it is designed to make the college experience more interactive, informative, and welcoming.

## Project Goal
The main purpose of this robot is to support freshers and other users by answering their questions about the university. It can help with information related to:

- college details
- admission and fees
- campus information
- courses and departments
- general student doubts

## Main Features
- Welcomes everyone politely with greetings such as good morning, good afternoon, and good night
- Talks with users in a friendly and helpful way
- Provides information about college events that have taken place in the past
- Shares links and details of college social media platforms such as Instagram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, and Twitter
- Gives information about Parul University leadership, including the owner, president, vice president, HOD, dean, and principal
- Provides details about all college buildings and campus locations
- Offers college maps for easier navigation

## What the Robot Can Do
- Assist new students with basic college guidance
- Answer common questions about fees, campus, and courses
- Help users discover past events and college activities
- Provide contact and social media information
- Guide students and visitors to the right buildings and areas

## Who It Helps
- Freshers
- Students
- Parents
- Visitors
- Prospective students

## Vision
This robot will act as a digital mentor for the college, making it easier for people to get information quickly and feel welcomed in the university environment.

## AI Capabilities
- Voice recognition (speech-to-text)
- Text-to-speech with natural voice
- Multi-language support (English, Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi)
- Personalized conversations
- Follow-up questions based on previous interactions
- Emotion detection from voice or facial expressions
